Tutorial

Before getting started, this tutorial level will give you a quick overview of how the game is played.

At the start of each level, a message box like this will be shown that lists the types of enemies in that level, along with a brief description of their behavior. After reviewing the enemies, click the start button at the bottom to begin the level.

Red Mine

Moves quickly towards your ship and attempts to collide with it. Causes a large amount of damage on impact. The blast radius from the explosion can also damage your ship or enemy ships.

Small Fighter

Moves at random. Fires lasers at a fast rate.
