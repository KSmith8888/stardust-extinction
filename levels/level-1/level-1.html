Level 1 Enemies

Red Mine

Moves quickly towards your ship and attempts to collide with it. Causes a large amount of damage on impact. The blast radius from the explosion can also damage your ship or enemy ships.

Blue Mine

Moves slowly towards your ship and attempts to collide with it. On impact, your ship will be disabled and unable to move or fire for a while.

Small Fighter

Moves at random. Fires lasers at a fast rate.

Small Racer

Enemy scout ship. Moves extremely quickly and does not engage. If destroyed, your ship gets a weapon upgrade for the remainder of the level.
